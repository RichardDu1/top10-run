Anaplan is a market leader in connected planning, offering robust modeling and reporting for finance teams. It integrates deeply with ERPs like Oracle, SAP, and NetSuite, enabling automated data flows for P&L, Balance Sheet, and Cash Flow reports. While its AI capabilities are not as conversational as Drivetrain or Pigment, Anaplan's strength lies in its scalability and complex modeling. It's ideal for large enterprises that need to manage multiple scenarios and consolidate data across business units.
