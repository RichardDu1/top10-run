Drivetrain is an AI-native FP&A platform that excels at automating financial reporting. It connects directly to ERPs like NetSuite and SAP, ingests data in real-time, and allows users to build P&L, Balance Sheet, and Cash Flow statements with drag-and-drop. Its standout feature is Conversational Insights – you can ask questions like 'Why did gross margin drop?' and get a natural language explanation with supporting charts. The anomaly detection automatically flags unusual transactions. Best for mid-market to enterprise companies that want to reduce manual reporting effort.
