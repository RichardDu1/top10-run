Choosing the right AI resume builder can save hours and improve interview chances. This ranking is based on strict pricing data and verified user feedback.

### 1. Rezi
**Best for**: Job seekers wanting an ATS-optimized resume with minimal manual input.
**Pricing**: Free tier (limited exports); Pro starts at $29/mo (billed monthly) or $9/mo (billed annually).
- **Pros**:
  - Strong ATS keyword targeting and scoring.
  - AI writes bullet points based on job descriptions.
- **Cons**:
  - Free version only allows one resume and limited exports.
  - Design templates are basic and not highly customizable.

### 2. Kickresume
**Best for**: Users who want a balance of design templates and AI writing assistance.
**Pricing**: Free tier (watermarked); Premium starts at $12/mo (billed annually) or $29/mo (billed monthly).
- **Pros**:
  - Large library of professional, modern templates.
  - AI writer can generate content from a job title.
- **Cons**:
  - Free exports include a watermark.
  - AI suggestions can sometimes be generic.

### 3. Zety
**Best for**: Users who prioritize design and step-by-step guidance.
**Pricing**: No free plan; 14-day trial for $2.95, then $25.90/mo (billed monthly).
- **Pros**:
  - Excellent, modern design templates.
  - Very intuitive builder with real-time tips.
- **Cons**:
  - No permanent free tier; requires payment to download.
  - Pricing is higher than many competitors.

### 4. Teal
**Best for**: Active job seekers managing multiple applications.
**Pricing**: Free tier (limited); Premium starts at $29/mo (billed monthly) or $14/mo (billed annually).
- **Pros**:
  - Includes a job tracker and application manager.
  - AI analyzes job descriptions to match resume keywords.
- **Cons**:
  - Resume builder is part of a larger suite, can feel complex.
  - Free tier is very limited in features.

### 5. Enhancv
**Best for**: Professionals wanting a visually unique, non-traditional resume.
**Pricing**: Free tier (limited); Premium starts at $19/mo (billed monthly) or $9/mo (billed annually).
- **Pros**:
  - Unique, modern design templates that stand out.
  - AI provides content suggestions based on your experience.
- **Cons**:
  - Designs may not be ATS-friendly for all industries.
  - Free version only allows one resume.

### 6. Resume.io
**Best for**: Users who want a simple, fast builder with reliable templates.
**Pricing**: Free tier (limited preview); Premium starts at $24.95/mo (billed monthly) or $9.95/mo (billed annually).
- **Pros**:
  - Very easy to use with a clean interface.
  - Templates are professional and widely compatible.
- **Cons**:
  - Free tier only shows a preview; must pay to export.
  - AI features are less advanced than competitors.

### 7. Novoresume
**Best for**: Entry-level candidates and career changers needing simple templates.
**Pricing**: Free tier (limited); Premium starts at $19.99/mo (billed monthly) or $9.99/mo (billed annually).
- **Pros**:
  - Clean, modern templates that are ATS-friendly.
  - Simple drag-and-drop interface.
- **Cons**:
  - AI writing assistant is basic.
  - Free version has limited color and font options.

### 8. Jobscan
**Best for**: Optimizing an existing resume for a specific job description.
**Pricing**: Free tier (limited scans); Premium starts at $49.95/mo (billed monthly) or $19.95/mo (billed annually).
- **Pros**:
  - Best-in-class ATS keyword matching and analysis.
  - Provides specific suggestions to improve match rate.
- **Cons**:
  - Not a traditional resume builder; focuses on optimization.
  - Pricing is high for the core resume builder feature.

### 9. MyPerfectResume
**Best for**: Users who want a guided, fill-in-the-blank experience.
**Pricing**: Free tier (limited preview); Premium starts at $19.99/mo (billed monthly) or $9.99/mo (billed annually).
- **Pros**:
  - Very easy to use with step-by-step prompts.
  - Large selection of industry-specific templates.
- **Cons**:
  - Free tier requires payment to download.
  - AI content can be repetitive.

### 10. VisualCV
**Best for**: Freelancers and creatives who need a portfolio-style resume.
**Pricing**: Free tier (limited); Pro starts at $15/mo (billed monthly) or $9/mo (billed annually).
- **Pros**:
  - Supports adding portfolio links and media.
  - Clean, modern templates.
- **Cons**:
  - AI writing features are minimal.
  - Less focus on ATS optimization.

**Conclusion**: For most job seekers, **Rezi** offers the best combination of powerful AI writing, ATS optimization, and a usable free tier. Its focus on matching your resume to specific job descriptions gives it a clear edge in getting past automated filters.
