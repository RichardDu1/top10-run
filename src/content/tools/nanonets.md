Nanonets is a user-friendly AI OCR platform that lets you train custom extraction models without coding. It supports invoice processing with fields like vendor, date, total, and line items. The free tier includes 500 pages per month, making it ideal for small businesses. Integrates with Zapier, QuickBooks, and Xero.
