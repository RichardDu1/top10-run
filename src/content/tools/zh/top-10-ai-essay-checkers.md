Choosing the right AI essay checker is critical for students and professionals who need to verify originality, grammar, and AI-generated content. Below is a strict ranking based on real-world pricing and feature data.

### 1. Turnitin
**Best for**: Academic institutions requiring plagiarism detection and AI writing detection.
**Pricing**: Institutional licensing only; no direct consumer pricing (estimated $3-5 per student per year).
- **Pros**:
  - Industry-standard plagiarism database with 99+ billion web pages and 1.7 billion student papers.
  - Integrated AI writing detection (Originality Check) with 98% accuracy.
- **Cons**:
  - Not available for individual purchase; requires school or university subscription.

### 2. Grammarly
**Best for**: General grammar, style, and tone checking with AI detection.
**Pricing**: Free tier available; Premium starts at $12/mo (annual); Business starts at $15/mo (annual).
- **Pros**:
  - Real-time grammar and spelling correction across 500,000+ apps and websites.
  - AI detection (Grammarly Authorship) identifies AI-generated text with 99% accuracy.
- **Cons**:
  - Plagiarism checker only available in Premium ($12/mo) and limited to ProQuest database.
  - AI detection is a beta feature and may flag human-written text.

### 3. Copyscape
**Best for**: Web plagiarism detection for content creators and SEO professionals.
**Pricing**: Pay-as-you-go at $0.03 per search (100 words); Premium API at $0.05 per search.
- **Pros**:
  - Searches the entire public web, not just indexed pages.
  - No false positives from common phrases; high precision.
- **Cons**:
  - No grammar or style checking; purely plagiarism detection.
  - No AI writing detection feature.

### 4. QuillBot
**Best for**: Paraphrasing and grammar checking with a built-in plagiarism detector.
**Pricing**: Free tier (limited words); Premium starts at $9.95/mo (annual) or $19.95/mo (monthly).
- **Pros**:
  - Integrated paraphrasing tool with 7 modes (Standard, Fluency, Formal, etc.).
  - Plagiarism checker compares against 60+ billion web pages.
- **Cons**:
  - AI detection is not a native feature; relies on third-party integration.
  - Free tier limits plagiarism checks to 20 pages per month.

### 5. Originality.ai
**Best for**: AI content detection and plagiarism checking for web publishers.
**Pricing**: Pay-as-you-go at $0.01 per 100 words; Monthly plans start at $14.95 (30,000 credits).
- **Pros**:
  - Detects AI-generated text from GPT-4, Claude, Gemini, and others with 99%+ accuracy.
  - Includes plagiarism check and readability scoring.
- **Cons**:
  - No grammar or style suggestions.
  - Requires manual upload or API integration; no browser extension.

### 6. Scribbr
**Best for**: Academic proofreading and plagiarism checking with human review.
**Pricing**: Plagiarism check starts at $19.95 (up to 7,500 words); Proofreading starts at $0.10 per word.
- **Pros**:
  - Uses Turnitin's plagiarism database for academic accuracy.
  - Offers human proofreading services with 24-hour turnaround.
- **Cons**:
  - No AI detection feature; only plagiarism and grammar.
  - Pricing is per-word, making it expensive for long essays.

### 7. ProWritingAid
**Best for**: In-depth grammar and style analysis for writers.
**Pricing**: Free tier (limited); Premium starts at $12/mo (annual) or $30/mo (monthly).
- **Pros**:
  - 20+ writing reports (grammar, style, readability, overused words).
  - Integrates with Word, Google Docs, Scrivener, and browser.
- **Cons**:
  - Plagiarism checker is an add-on at $10/mo (Premium+ plan).
  - No AI writing detection.

### 8. GPTZero
**Best for**: Detecting AI-generated text in academic submissions.
**Pricing**: Free tier (5,000 characters per scan); Pro starts at $15/mo (100,000 characters).
- **Pros**:
  - Specialized in AI detection for ChatGPT, GPT-4, and other models.
  - Provides sentence-level highlighting of AI-generated content.
- **Cons**:
  - No plagiarism or grammar checking.
  - Accuracy drops below 90% for heavily edited AI text.

### 9. Sapling
**Best for**: Grammar and autocomplete for customer support and business writing.
**Pricing**: Free tier (limited); Pro starts at $25/mo (annual) or $30/mo (monthly).
- **Pros**:
  - Real-time grammar and spelling correction with context-aware suggestions.
  - AI detection (Sapling Authorship) identifies AI-generated text.
- **Cons**:
  - Plagiarism checker is not available; only grammar and AI detection.
  - Limited to English language only.

### 10. Writer.com
**Best for**: Enterprise AI writing and brand consistency.
**Pricing**: Team plan starts at $18/mo per user (annual); Enterprise custom pricing.
- **Pros**:
  - AI detection (Writer AI Guardrails) with 95%+ accuracy for GPT-4.
  - Plagiarism checker integrated with web and internal databases.
- **Cons**:
  - No free tier; only paid plans.
  - Primarily designed for business teams, not individual students.

The #1 overall winner is **Turnitin** due to its unmatched academic plagiarism database and integrated AI detection, though it is only available through institutions. For individual users, **Grammarly** offers the best balance of grammar, plagiarism, and AI detection at $12/mo.
