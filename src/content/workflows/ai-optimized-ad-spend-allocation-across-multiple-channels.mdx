## Introduction

As a paid media manager, you juggle multiple channels—Google Ads, Meta, LinkedIn, and more. Manually optimizing ad spend is time-consuming and often suboptimal. This workflow leverages AI tools to automate budget allocation, ensuring each channel gets the right spend based on performance.

## Prerequisites

- Access to your ad accounts (Google Ads, Meta Business Suite, etc.)
- A data integration tool (e.g., Supermetrics) to pull data into a central dashboard
- An AI optimization tool (e.g., Adzooma or Optmyzr)

## Step 1: Centralize Your Data

Use **Supermetrics** to connect all your ad platforms to a single spreadsheet or BI tool (e.g., Google Sheets, Looker Studio). Set up automated daily pulls of key metrics: spend, impressions, clicks, conversions, and cost per acquisition (CPA).

## Step 2: Define Performance Rules

In your AI tool (e.g., **Adzooma**), create rules based on your KPIs. For example:
- If a channel’s ROAS drops below 3x for 3 consecutive days, reduce its budget by 10%.
- If a channel’s CPA is 20% lower than target, increase budget by 15%.

## Step 3: Set Up Automated Budget Adjustments

Use **Optmyzr** or **Adzooma** to automate these rules. Connect your ad accounts and enable the AI to make real-time budget shifts. For instance, if Google Ads outperforms LinkedIn, the tool will reallocate funds accordingly.

## Step 4: Monitor and Refine

Review performance weekly. Use the AI’s reporting to identify trends. Adjust rules as needed—e.g., add seasonality or campaign-level exceptions.

## Step 5: Scale with Confidence

Once the system runs smoothly, expand to more channels (e.g., TikTok, Pinterest). The AI will handle the complexity, freeing you to focus on strategy.

## Conclusion

By implementing this AI-optimized workflow, you’ll reduce manual work, improve ROAS, and stay ahead of the competition. Start with a pilot channel and scale from there.
