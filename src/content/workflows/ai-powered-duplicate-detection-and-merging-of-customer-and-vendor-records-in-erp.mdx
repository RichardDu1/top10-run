## Overview

Duplicate customer and vendor records in ERP systems cause billing errors, misdirected shipments, and inaccurate reporting. This guide leverages AI-powered tools to automatically detect and merge duplicates, ensuring clean master data.

## Prerequisites

- Access to your ERP system (e.g., SAP, Oracle, Microsoft Dynamics)
- Export of customer and vendor master data (CSV or Excel)
- Accounts on Energent.ai and DedupFuzzy (free tiers available)

## Step 1: Export Master Data from ERP

Export customer and vendor records as CSV files. Include fields like Name, Address, Phone, Email, Tax ID, and ERP ID.

## Step 2: Upload Data to DedupFuzzy for Initial Scan

1. Go to [DedupFuzzy](https://dedupfuzzy.com) and upload your CSV.
2. Use AI-assisted matching to find near-duplicates. Adjust similarity thresholds (e.g., 85% for names, 90% for addresses).
3. Review the list of potential duplicates. Mark pairs for merging.
4. Export the deduplication report as CSV.

## Step 3: Deploy Energent.ai for Automated Deduplication

1. Sign up at [Energent.ai](https://energent.ai) and install the AI teammate on your desktop.
2. Connect the AI to your ERP system (no-code integration via screen scraping).
3. Configure rules: The AI will monitor new and existing records for duplicates.
4. When a duplicate is detected, the AI presents options: merge, delete, or review.
5. Approve actions; the AI learns from your decisions over time.

## Step 4: Merge Records in ERP

- For each duplicate pair, decide which record to keep (master) and which to merge/delete.
- Use the AI's merge function to consolidate data: combine addresses, phone numbers, and transaction history.
- Update related records (orders, invoices) to point to the master record.

## Step 5: Validate and Monitor

- Run a sample check: query the ERP for duplicates using built-in tools.
- Set up weekly reports from Energent.ai on deduplication activity.
- Adjust AI sensitivity as needed.

## Best Practices

- Always backup data before merging.
- Involve sales and procurement teams to verify critical records.
- Schedule regular deduplication runs (e.g., monthly).

## Conclusion

By combining DedupFuzzy's initial scan with Energent.ai's continuous monitoring, you can maintain a clean ERP master data set, reducing errors and improving operational efficiency.
