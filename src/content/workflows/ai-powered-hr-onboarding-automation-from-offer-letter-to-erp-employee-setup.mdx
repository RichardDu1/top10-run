## Overview

This guide walks you through building an AI-powered onboarding workflow that automates the journey from offer letter to ERP employee setup. By integrating AI tools like ChatGPT for document generation, Zapier for workflow automation, and HR platforms like BambooHR or Gusto, you can eliminate manual data entry, reduce errors, and create a seamless experience for new hires.

## Prerequisites

- An HRIS or ERP system (e.g., BambooHR, Gusto, SAP SuccessFactors)
- A workflow automation tool (e.g., Zapier, Make)
- An AI assistant (e.g., ChatGPT, Claude)
- An e-signature tool (e.g., DocuSign, HelloSign)
- A document storage solution (e.g., Google Drive, Dropbox)

## Step 1: Automate Offer Letter Generation with AI

**Goal:** Generate a personalized offer letter instantly when a candidate accepts.

1. **Set up a trigger** in Zapier: When a new hire is added to your ATS (e.g., Greenhouse, Lever) or when a candidate status changes to "Accepted", trigger the workflow.
2. **Use ChatGPT** to generate the offer letter: Pass candidate details (name, role, start date, salary) to ChatGPT via Zapier's OpenAI integration. Prompt example: "Generate a professional offer letter for [Name] for the position of [Role] starting on [Date] with a salary of [Salary]. Include standard clauses about at-will employment and background check."
3. **Save the letter** to Google Drive or DocuSign template.
4. **Send for e-signature** via DocuSign or HelloSign automatically.

**Tools:** Zapier, ChatGPT, DocuSign, Google Drive

## Step 2: Collect New Hire Documents via AI-Powered Forms

**Goal:** Automate collection of W-4, I-9, direct deposit, and other forms.

1. **Create a smart form** using Typeform or Jotform with conditional logic.
2. **Integrate with Zapier**: When the offer letter is signed, trigger an email to the new hire with a link to the form.
3. **Use AI to pre-fill** common fields: ChatGPT can extract data from the offer letter and populate form fields via Zapier.
4. **Store documents** in a secure folder (e.g., Google Drive) and notify HR for review.

**Tools:** Typeform, Zapier, ChatGPT, Google Drive

## Step 3: Automate Task Assignment and Training

**Goal:** Assign onboarding tasks and training modules automatically.

1. **Create a task list** in your project management tool (e.g., Asana, Trello) or HRIS.
2. **Use Zapier** to create tasks based on the new hire's role and department. For example, IT setup, benefits enrollment, buddy assignment.
3. **Integrate with LMS** (e.g., TalentLMS, Docebo) to assign mandatory training courses.
4. **Send personalized welcome messages** using ChatGPT to draft emails or Slack messages.

**Tools:** Asana, Zapier, ChatGPT, TalentLMS

## Step 4: Sync Employee Data to ERP/HRIS

**Goal:** Automatically create employee record in your ERP (e.g., SAP, Oracle, NetSuite) or HRIS (e.g., BambooHR, Gusto).

1. **Map fields** between your onboarding form and ERP fields (name, SSN, address, pay rate, department).
2. **Use Zapier** to create or update employee record in your ERP when all documents are collected and signed.
3. **Set up error handling**: If data is missing, send an alert to HR.
4. **Trigger payroll setup** in tools like Gusto or ADP.

**Tools:** Zapier, BambooHR, Gusto, ADP

## Step 5: Monitor and Optimize

**Goal:** Track onboarding completion and gather feedback.

1. **Set up dashboards** in your HRIS or BI tool (e.g., Tableau) to monitor time-to-completion.
2. **Use AI to analyze feedback**: After 30 days, send a survey via Typeform and use ChatGPT to summarize sentiment.
3. **Iterate**: Adjust triggers and templates based on common issues.

**Tools:** Tableau, Typeform, ChatGPT

## Conclusion

By following this workflow, you can reduce onboarding admin time by 80%, ensure compliance, and improve new hire experience. Start small with offer letter automation and expand step by step.
