## Overview

Personalized email sequences based on website browsing history allow you to send the right message at the right time. By tracking which pages a visitor views, you can tailor follow-up emails to their specific interests. This guide uses AI to automate content creation and personalization.

## Prerequisites

- A website with tracking (e.g., HubSpot tracking code)
- An email marketing platform (HubSpot)
- AI content generation tool (ChatGPT)
- Data enrichment tool (Clearbit)
- Automation tool (Zapier)

## Step 1: Set Up Website Tracking

Install HubSpot tracking code on your website to capture page visits. In HubSpot, go to **Settings > Tracking & Analytics > Tracking Code** and copy the code into your site's header.

## Step 2: Enrich Visitor Data with Clearbit

Connect Clearbit to HubSpot to append firmographic data (company size, industry) to anonymous visitors. This helps segment leads. Use Zapier to automate: when a new contact is created in HubSpot, enrich with Clearbit.

## Step 3: Define Browsing-Based Segments

In HubSpot, create lists based on pages visited. For example:
- **Pricing page visitors**: High intent
- **Blog readers**: Educational content
- **Case study viewers**: Consideration stage

## Step 4: Create AI-Generated Email Templates

Use ChatGPT to draft personalized email sequences for each segment. Prompt example: "Write a 3-email sequence for a SaaS prospect who visited the pricing page. Tone: professional, personalized. Include placeholders for company name and industry."

## Step 5: Build Automated Workflows in HubSpot

Create workflows triggered by page visits:
1. **Trigger**: Contact visits pricing page
2. **Action**: Add to "Pricing Interest" list
3. **Action**: Send email sequence (from Step 4)
4. **Delay**: 1 day between emails

## Step 6: Personalize with Dynamic Content

Use HubSpot personalization tokens (e.g., `{{contact.firstname}}`, `{{contact.company}}`) and Clearbit data to insert company-specific details. For deeper personalization, use ChatGPT to generate unique subject lines based on browsing history via Zapier.

## Step 7: Test and Optimize

A/B test subject lines, send times, and email length. Monitor open rates and click-through rates. Adjust AI prompts based on performance.

## Conclusion

By combining website tracking, AI content generation, and automation, you can deliver hyper-personalized email sequences that resonate with each prospect. This workflow scales personalization without manual effort.
