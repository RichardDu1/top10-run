## Overview

Automated expense report auditing using AI eliminates manual checks, reduces errors, and ensures policy compliance. This guide walks you through setting up an AI-driven audit workflow using Rydoo's Smart Audit and AppZen's real-time fraud detection.

## Prerequisites

- An expense management system (e.g., Rydoo, Concur)
- Admin access to configure policies
- Integration with accounting software (e.g., QuickBooks, NetSuite)

## Step 1: Define Your Expense Policy

Before automation, clearly define your expense policy rules. Common rules include:
- Maximum amounts per category (e.g., meals $50)
- Required receipt uploads for expenses over $25
- Prohibited vendors or expense types

Document these rules in a policy document that your AI tool can reference.

## Step 2: Choose an AI Audit Tool

Select a tool that fits your needs. Two top options:

- **Rydoo Smart Audit**: Uses AI to automatically flag out-of-policy claims. Integrates with Rydoo's expense management platform.
- **AppZen**: Enterprise-grade AI that audits 100% of expenses, detects duplicates, policy violations, and fraud. Integrates with major ERP systems.

For this guide, we'll use **Rydoo** as it's easier to set up for small to mid-sized businesses.

## Step 3: Configure Policy Rules in Rydoo

1. Log into your Rydoo admin console.
2. Navigate to **Settings > Expense Policies**.
3. Create a new policy or edit existing.
4. Add rules:
   - **Spending Limits**: Set per-category caps.
   - **Receipt Requirements**: Require receipts for amounts > $25.
   - **Blacklisted Vendors**: Add vendors not allowed.
5. Enable **Smart Audit** toggle to activate AI checks.
6. Save policy.

## Step 4: Integrate with Accounting Software

Connect Rydoo to your accounting system for seamless sync:
1. Go to **Integrations** in Rydoo.
2. Select your accounting software (e.g., QuickBooks).
3. Authorize the connection.
4. Map expense categories to your chart of accounts.

This ensures approved expenses flow automatically into your books.

## Step 5: Test the Audit Workflow

Submit a few test expense reports:
- One that complies with policy
- One that violates a rule (e.g., over limit)
- One with a duplicate receipt

Check that:
- Compliant reports are auto-approved.
- Violations are flagged with a reason.
- Duplicates are detected and blocked.

## Step 6: Review and Refine

Monitor the AI's decisions for the first week. Adjust policy rules if false positives occur. Rydoo's Smart Audit learns from corrections, improving accuracy over time.

## Step 7: Go Live and Monitor

Once satisfied, enable the workflow for all employees. Set up weekly reports to review flagged items and audit logs.

## Best Practices

- Regularly update policy rules as company needs change.
- Train employees on the new automated process.
- Use AI audit logs for compliance reporting.

## Conclusion

Automating expense report auditing with AI reduces manual effort, ensures policy compliance, and provides real-time fraud detection. With tools like Rydoo and AppZen, you can achieve 100% audit coverage with minimal setup.
