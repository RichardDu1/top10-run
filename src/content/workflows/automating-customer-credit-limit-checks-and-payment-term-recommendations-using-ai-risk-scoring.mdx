## Overview

This workflow automates customer credit limit checks and payment term recommendations using AI risk scoring. By integrating a credit decisioning platform with your CRM or ERP, you can instantly assess risk and suggest optimal payment terms, reducing manual effort and improving cash flow.

## Prerequisites

- Access to a CRM or ERP system (e.g., Salesforce, NetSuite)
- API access for data integration
- A credit decisioning AI tool (e.g., Credit Decisioning AI)
- A collections automation tool (e.g., Anytime Collect)

## Step 1: Set Up Credit Decisioning AI

1. Sign up for [Credit Decisioning AI](https://creditdecisioning.ai) and create an account.
2. Configure your risk scoring model by uploading historical payment data and credit bureau reports.
3. Define credit limit thresholds (e.g., low risk: $50,000, medium risk: $20,000, high risk: $5,000).
4. Set payment term recommendations (e.g., low risk: Net 60, medium risk: Net 30, high risk: Net 15 or prepayment).

## Step 2: Integrate with Your CRM/ERP

1. Use the API provided by Credit Decisioning AI to connect with your CRM (e.g., Salesforce).
2. Map customer fields (e.g., company name, revenue, industry) to the AI model inputs.
3. Set up a webhook or scheduled job to trigger credit checks on new customer creation or order placement.

## Step 3: Automate Credit Limit Checks

1. In your CRM, create a custom field for "Credit Limit" and "Payment Terms".
2. When a new customer is added or an order exceeds a threshold, the AI model automatically scores the customer.
3. The system updates the credit limit and recommended payment terms in the CRM.
4. If the score is high risk, trigger an alert to the credit manager for manual review.

## Step 4: Integrate with Collections Automation

1. Connect [Anytime Collect](https://anytimecollect.com) to your CRM.
2. Sync customer credit limits and payment terms from the CRM to Anytime Collect.
3. Configure automated dunning emails based on payment terms (e.g., send reminder 5 days before due date).
4. Set up escalation rules for overdue accounts based on risk score.

## Step 5: Monitor and Optimize

1. Review AI scoring accuracy monthly by comparing predicted risk vs actual payment behavior.
2. Adjust model parameters or thresholds as needed.
3. Use dashboards in Credit Decisioning AI to track approval rates, average credit limits, and days sales outstanding (DSO).

## Conclusion

By automating credit limit checks and payment term recommendations with AI risk scoring, you can reduce manual review time by 80%, accelerate customer onboarding, and improve cash flow. This workflow is scalable and can be customized to fit your business rules.
