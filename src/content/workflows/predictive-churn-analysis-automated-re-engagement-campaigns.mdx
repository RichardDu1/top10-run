## Overview

This workflow combines predictive churn analysis with automated re-engagement campaigns. You'll use HubSpot's predictive scoring (or a custom model via Zapier + Retently) to identify at-risk customers, then trigger personalized email sequences and in-app messages to win them back.

## Prerequisites

- HubSpot CRM (Professional or Enterprise for predictive scoring)
- Retently account (for NPS surveys and churn prediction)
- Zapier account (for automation)
- Basic knowledge of HubSpot workflows and Zapier

## Step 1: Set Up Predictive Churn Scoring in HubSpot

1. In HubSpot, navigate to **Contacts** > **Lists** and create a new active list of customers with a contract or subscription.
2. Go to **Automation** > **Predictive Scoring** and enable the churn prediction model. HubSpot will automatically score contacts based on engagement, support tickets, product usage (if integrated), and email activity.
3. Create a custom property `Churn Risk Score` (0-100) and map the predictive score to it.
4. Set up a workflow that triggers when `Churn Risk Score` > 70: add contact to a static list "High Churn Risk".

## Step 2: Integrate Retently for NPS and Churn Signals

1. In Retently, create a new NPS survey campaign targeting customers who have been active for at least 30 days.
2. Enable Retently's churn prediction feature (uses response patterns and engagement).
3. Use Zapier to connect Retently to HubSpot: when a customer submits a low NPS (0-6) or is flagged as "at risk" by Retently, create/update a HubSpot contact property `Retently Churn Flag` = true.

## Step 3: Build Automated Re-engagement Campaigns in HubSpot

1. Create a HubSpot email sequence called "Re-engagement Flow".
2. Set the enrollment trigger: contact is in "High Churn Risk" list OR `Retently Churn Flag` is true.
3. Design the sequence:
   - **Day 1**: Personalized email from CSM offering a free consultation or product walkthrough.
   - **Day 3**: Case study relevant to their use case.
   - **Day 7**: Special offer (e.g., discount on next renewal, extended trial).
   - **Day 14**: Final check-in with a survey link to gather feedback.
4. Add a conditional branch: if contact opens email or clicks link, remove from sequence and add to "Engaged" list.

## Step 4: Trigger In-App Messages (Optional)

If you use HubSpot's Conversations or a tool like Intercom, create an in-app message that appears when a high-risk customer logs in. Use Zapier to send a webhook to Intercom when a contact is added to "High Churn Risk".

## Step 5: Monitor and Optimize

1. Create a dashboard in HubSpot showing churn risk score distribution, re-engagement campaign performance (open rate, click rate, conversion rate).
2. Review Retently NPS trends monthly.
3. Adjust scoring thresholds and email copy based on results.

## Automation Summary

- **Trigger**: High churn risk score (HubSpot) or low NPS (Retently)
- **Actions**: Add to re-engagement sequence, send email series, update CRM properties
- **Tools**: HubSpot, Retently, Zapier

## Troubleshooting

- If predictive scoring isn't available, use a manual scoring system based on login frequency, support tickets, and email engagement.
- Ensure Zapier connections are authenticated and test each step.

## Conclusion

This workflow automates the detection and re-engagement of at-risk customers, freeing your CS team to focus on high-touch interactions. With continuous optimization, you can significantly reduce churn.
