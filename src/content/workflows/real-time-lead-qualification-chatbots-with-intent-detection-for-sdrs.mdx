## Real-Time Lead Qualification Chatbots with Intent Detection

### Overview
This workflow helps SDRs automate the initial lead qualification process using a chatbot that detects buyer intent in real-time. By integrating Drift (or a similar chatbot) with HubSpot CRM and Zapier, you can instantly capture, qualify, and route leads without manual effort.

### Prerequisites
- A Drift account (free tier available)
- A HubSpot CRM account (free tier available)
- A Zapier account (free tier available)

### Step 1: Set Up Your Drift Chatbot
1. Log in to Drift and navigate to **Chatbots**.
2. Create a new chatbot named "Lead Qualifier".
3. Add a welcome message: "Hi! I'm here to help. Are you looking for [Product/Service]?"
4. Configure intent detection by adding keywords like "pricing", "demo", "trial", "buy".
5. Set up a qualification flow: ask questions like "What's your company size?", "What's your role?", "What's your timeline?"
6. Use Drift's built-in lead scoring to assign points based on answers.

### Step 2: Connect Drift to HubSpot via Zapier
1. In Zapier, create a new Zap.
2. Trigger: **Drift - New Conversation**.
3. Action: **HubSpot - Create/Update Contact**.
4. Map Drift fields (email, name, company) to HubSpot contact properties.
5. Add a second action: **HubSpot - Create Deal** (optional, for qualified leads).
6. Test the Zap to ensure data flows correctly.

### Step 3: Configure Intent Detection Rules
1. In Drift, go to **Rules** and create a new rule: "High Intent Lead".
2. Condition: If conversation contains keywords like "pricing" or "demo" AND lead score > 50.
3. Action: Send Slack notification to SDR team and tag lead as "Hot".
4. Create a second rule: "Low Intent Lead" for generic inquiries.
5. Set up email alerts for high-intent leads via Zapier (Drift → Email).

### Step 4: Test and Optimize
1. Simulate a conversation as a prospect with high intent.
2. Verify that the chatbot captures the lead, scores it, and triggers the correct actions.
3. Review conversation logs in Drift to refine keywords and questions.
4. Adjust lead scoring thresholds based on conversion data.

### Step 5: Monitor and Iterate
1. Use HubSpot dashboards to track lead source (Drift) and conversion rates.
2. A/B test different chatbot messages and qualification questions.
3. Regularly update intent keywords based on real conversations.

### Troubleshooting
- **No leads flowing**: Check Zapier connection and ensure Drift conversations are active.
- **Wrong intent detection**: Refine keyword list and use negative keywords to filter out noise.
- **Low engagement**: Shorten chatbot messages and add quick reply buttons.

### Conclusion
This workflow reduces manual lead screening by 80%, allowing SDRs to focus on high-intent prospects. With real-time intent detection, you can respond instantly and increase conversion rates.
